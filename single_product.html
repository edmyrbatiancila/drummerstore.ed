Product Details

DW Drums have been producing iconic drum kits since being founded in 1972 in Santa Monica, California - This gorgeous collectors series kit is no different to the usual expectation. The most popular wood for drum making - Maple is a hardwood that produces maximum resonance, projection and attack. Combined with reinforced hoops, it can produce a more focused sound and is well suited for live and studio applications. This drum also features VTM (Vertical Low Timbre). In a VTL Shell, the outermost and innermost piles run vertically, rather than a traditionally horizontal configuration. The vertical grain places less tension on the shell, allowing it to vibrate more freely, resulting in the lowered fundamental pitch.
